# [OFFICIAL SOURCE] IELTS Speaking Evaluation Criteria (2024-2025 Updates)

## 1. Fluency and Coherence (FC)
- **Band 9:** Speaks fluently with rare hesitation. **Examiner Focus:** Logic and coherence are prioritized over sheer speed.
- **Band 8:** Fluency with occasional repetition. Coherent throughout.
- **Band 7:** Lengthy speech without loss of coherence; occasional language-related hesitation.

## 2. Lexical Resource (LR)
- **Band 9:** Full flexibility and precision. **Examiner Focus:** Natural usage. Forced use of "big words" that don't fit perfectly can penalize candidates.
- **Band 8:** Skills in idiomatic vocabulary; precise meaning.
- **Band 7:** Awareness of style and collocation; effective paraphrasing.

## 3. Grammatical Range and Accuracy (GRA)
- **Band 9:** Full range of structures with precision. Error-free apart from minor slips.
- **Band 8:** Flexible range; majority of sentences are error-free.
- **Band 7:** Complex structures used with some flexibility.

## 4. Pronunciation (P)
- **Band 9:** Full range of features (stress, intonation, rhythm); effortless understanding.
- **Band 8:** Sustained flexible use of features; occasional lapses.
- **Band 7:** Positive control of stress and intonation.
