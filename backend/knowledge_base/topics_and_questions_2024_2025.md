# IELTS Speaking Topics & Questions (Extracted from 101 Topics & 2023-2024 PDF Guides)

## Part 1: Core & Seasonal Topics
- **Work/Study:** Do you work or are you a student? Why did you choose that subject/job? Plans for the future?
- **Hometown/Accommodation:** Do you live in a house or apartment? Has your hometown changed much since childhood?
- **Hobbies:** Favorite leisure activities? Do you prefer to spend time alone or with others?
- **Technology & Apps:** What apps do you mostly use? Are you interested in robots?
- **Colors:** Special meaning of colors in your culture? Favorite color for clothes?
- **Transportation:** Most popular means of transport in your town? Public transport vs. private?
- **Gifts:** Handmade gifts? Giving vs. receiving?

## Part 2: Categorized Long Turns (101 Topics Sample)
- **People:**
    - Describe someone who makes you laugh.
    - Describe a young person you enjoy spending time with.
    - Describe an energetic person you know.
- **Places:**
    - Describe a garden or outdoor space you enjoy.
    - Describe a foreign country you are interested in.
    - Describe a place that impressed you (building/view).
- **Objects/Things:**
    - Describe your first school bag.
    - Describe a useful electronic device you would like to own.
    - Describe a piece of art you like.
- **Events/Occasions:**
    - Describe a journey that didn't go as planned.
    - Describe a time you gave good advice.
    - Describe a difficult choice you once made.

## Part 3: Abstract Discussion (Thematic Follow-ups)
- **Coherence & Logic:** How has technology changed the way people communicate? (Focus on logical progression).
- **Globalization:** Impact on traditional festivals and holidays.
- **Environment:** Sustainable solutions and government policies.
- **Education:** Reward systems vs. intrinsic motivation in children.
- **Decision Making:** Factors influencing big life changes (Career, Marriage).
